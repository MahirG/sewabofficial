About Sewab Travel Designers

Pilgrimage, perfected with care and trust.

Sewab Travel Designers is an Ethiopian Umrah package organizer dedicated to one purpose: making your spiritual journey effortless, dignified, and deeply meaningful. From the first inquiry in Addis Ababa to your return home, every detail is handled by people who understand both the rites and the responsibility.

We arrange visas, flights, hotels within walking distance of the Haram, ground transport, and knowledgeable guides — so you can give your heart fully to worship.

  • Fully managed door-to-door pilgrimages
  • Hotels close to Masjid al-Haram & the Prophet's Mosque
  • Experienced Amharic-speaking guides throughout
